    ATS & Resume Optimization Manual

    Structural Directives

    Use Standard Section Headers: Stick to universal conventions like "Work Experience", "Education", and "Skills". Custom titles like "Where I've Been" trick structural regex parsers.

    Quantify Everything: Replace ambiguous tasks with metrics. Change "Responsible for managing servers" to "Maintained 99.98% uptime metrics across 14 enterprise server topologies."

    Critical Pitfalls to Avoid

    No Charts/Graphics for Content: While charts look good to humans, text trapped inside images or graphs is completely invisible to fundamental system text layers.

    Avoid Header/Footer Text Data: Avoid putting crucial contact details inside the literal file margin properties. Put it cleanly at the top of the body flow.
